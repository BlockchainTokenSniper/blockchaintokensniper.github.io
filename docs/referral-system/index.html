Referral System (V3)

BlockchainTokenSniper V3 features a rewarding referral system, allowing you to earn by inviting new users to the platform. All referral activities are managed directly through the @btsv3bot Telegram bot.

How It Works

  1. Get Your Referral Link:
    • Interact with the @btsv3bot on Telegram.
    • Navigate to the "Referral" or "My Account" section in the bot's menu.
    • The bot will provide you with your unique referral link (e.g., https://t.me/btsv3bot?start=YOUR_REFERRAL_ID).
  2. Share Your Link: Share this link with friends or your community.
  3. New User Signs Up: When a new user starts the @btsv3bot using your referral link, they become your referred user.

Self-referrals are not permitted. If a user attempts to use their own ID as a referral ID, it will be ignored.

Referral Benefits for V3

As a referrer in BTS V3, you can benefit in multiple ways:

  • Tier Purchase Discount for Referred Users:
    • When your referred user purchases a BTS V3 tier, they will receive a discount.
    • Users get a discount of 10% when they subscribe to or purchase a tier.
  • Commission on Tier Purchases:
    • You earn a commission on the original price of the tier purchased by your referred user.
    • Referrers earn a commission of 20% of tier purchases / subscriptions from users they've referred.
    • Commissions are paid out automatically to your configured wallet after the referred user's payment is confirmed.
  • Commission on Trading Fees:
    • You earn a percentage of the trading fees incurred by your referred users from their profitable snipes.
    • Referrers earn a trading fee commission percentage of 25%.
    • These commissions are tracked and can be claimed/withdrawn through the bot.

Managing Your Referrals and Earnings

The @btsv3bot provides a dedicated "Referral" section where you can:

  • View your unique referral link.
  • Configure your desired discount percentage for new users (if this feature is enabled for your account).
  • Track the number of users you've referred.
  • View your total earnings from tier purchase commissions (paid out automatically).
  • View and claim your accumulated earnings from trading fee commissions.
  • See detailed logs of referral payouts.

Start sharing your referral link today and earn rewards with BlockchainTokenSniper V3!

All referral percentages and program details are subject to change. Please refer to the information within the @btsv3bot for the most current terms.
