How to Start Sniping with BTS V3

Getting started with BlockchainTokenSniper V3 is simple:

  1. Open Telegram and search for the official bot: @btsv3bot.
  2. Start a chat with the bot and follow the on-screen instructions for initial setup, including wallet configuration.
  3. To unlock the full power of BTS V3, you'll need to subscribe to a tier. The bot will guide you through the available tiers and the purchase process.

Once subscribed, you'll gain access to both the BlockchainLaunchSniper and BlockchainMultiSniper bots, along with all their features, managed conveniently through the Telegram interface.

BTS V3 Tiers and Fees

BlockchainTokenSniper V3 offers a flexible tier system to cater to different user needs, providing access to its powerful sniping and automated trading features.

Free Tier

  • Access to essential features of the BlockchainLaunchSniper and BlockchainMultiSniper bots.
  • Ideal for users looking to try out the bot or for occasional sniping.
  • A standard commission fee is applied to profitable trades. The current percentage can be found within the bot.

Pro Tier

  • Unlocks the full suite of advanced features for both sniping bots, including priority access to new updates and capabilities.
  • 0% commission on all profitable trades. Maximize your gains!
  • Pro Tier access can be obtained via:
    • Lifetime Purchase (PRO_LIFETIME): A one-time payment for permanent Pro access.
    • Subscription (PRO_MONTHLY): Recurring monthly payments for continuous Pro access. Your access remains active as long as the subscription is maintained (proTierExpires will indicate the next renewal date).
  • Pro users with active subscriptions (e.g., "PRO_EXPIRING") will have their access valid until their proTierExpires date.

Note: Specific pricing for Pro Tier options, current Free Tier commission rates, and detailed feature differences are available exclusively within the @btsv3bot Telegram bot. Please interact with the bot for the most up-to-date information and to manage your tier.
